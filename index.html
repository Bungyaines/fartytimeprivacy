Privacy Policy for FartyTime

Effective Date: 19th November 2025

Introduction

Farty ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

  • Audio Recordings: Users can record and upload audio clips ("farts") for sharing and rating.
  • User Interactions: We collect information on ratings, team activity, and remixes within the app.
  • Device Information: We may collect data such as device type, operating system, and anonymous analytics for improving performance.
  • Optional Contact Information: If you choose to contact us or sign up for updates, we may receive your email address or social profile.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Farty app and its features.
  • To allow sharing, rating, and remixing of audio clips.
  • To maintain a safe and enjoyable user experience.
  • For technical support and communication about our services.
  • To analyze usage and trends to enhance app functionality.

How We Share Information

  • We do not sell your information to third parties.
  • Your audio clips and interactions may be visible within the Farty community.
  • Service providers may help us host and operate our app, but they are bound to keep data confidential.
  • Law enforcement disclosures only as required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You may delete your audio clips and other contributions from within the app.
  • Contact us if you wish to request deletion of your account or personal data.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to help protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, so please use personal discretion when sharing content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Updates will be posted on this page, with a new effective date.
